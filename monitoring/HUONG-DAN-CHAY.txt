HUONG DAN CHAY — PC MONITOR (may chu + may nhan)
================================================

Tong quan
---------
  - MAY CHU: chay monitoring-server.exe — nhan du lieu + anh tu agent, luu SQLite + thu muc captures.
  - MAY NHAN (agent): chay pc-monitor-client.exe — gui du lieu ve may chu (cung token trong auth.token).

File auth.token
---------------
  - Noi dung 1 dong (hex) phai GIONG NHAU tren may chu va tren moi may agent.
  - May chu: file auth.token dat CUNG thu muc voi monitoring-server.exe (hoac dat BEARER_TOKEN trong server.env).
  - May nhan: copy auth.token vao CUNG thu muc voi pc-monitor-client.exe (trong bo may-nhan-goi-client).


=== A) CHAY BANG FILE .EXE (khuyen dung sau khi build) ===

1) Build (tren may dev, trong PowerShell, thu muc monitoring\deploy):

     .\build_apps.ps1

   Ket qua trong:  deploy\dist\
     - monitoring-server.exe
     - pc-monitor-client.exe  (agent may nhan — khong con dong goi pc-monitor-bot / -gui)
     - monitoring-alerter.exe  (neu build full)
     - auth.token  (neu chua co se duoc tao; giu file nay lam chuan)

   Tao bo gui may nhan:

     .\client-kit\tao_bo_cai_dat.ps1

   Ket qua:  deploy\may-nhan-goi-client\  (zip ca thu muc de gui).

2) May chu (server)

   - Copy deploy\dist\  len may chu (hoac chi can monitoring-server.exe + auth.token cung thu muc).
   - Mo firewall Windows: cho phep cong TCP 8010 (hoac cong ban dat trong bien PORT).
   - Double-click monitoring-server.exe  (hoac chay tu CMD/PowerShell trong dung thu muc).

   Mac dinh:
     - Lang nghe: 0.0.0.0:8010
     - Trang admin: http://<IP_MAY_CHU>:8010/admin
     - Agent gui ve: http://<IP_MAY_CHU>:8010/api/agent/data

   Tuy chon (bien moi truong hoac file server.env canh .exe):
     - PORT=8010
     - HOST=0.0.0.0
     - BEARER_TOKEN=...  (neu khong muon dung auth.token)
     - ADMIN_TOKEN=...   (tuy chon, rieng cho /admin; bo trong thi dung chung BEARER_TOKEN)

   Du lieu:
     - monitor.db   — cung thu muc .exe
     - captures\    — anh man hinh theo tung agent

   2b) Web admin (giao dien danh sach may + anh man hinh)

   - Mo trinh duyet (Chrome / Edge / Firefox) tren MAY BAT KY trong mang (hoac tren chinh may chu):
       http://<IP_MAY_CHU>:8010/admin
     Neu xem tren chinh may chu:  http://127.0.0.1:8010/admin

   - O truong "Token": dan NGUYEN DONG dau tien trong file auth.token (cung noi dung
     BEARER_TOKEN / ADMIN_TOKEN neu ban dat trong server.env).
     Neu da dat ADMIN_TOKEN rieng thi nhap ADMIN_TOKEN vao o nay de vao /admin.

   - Bam "Tai danh sach may": cot trai hien cac may da co agent; bam vao mot may de xem
     luoi anh man hinh ben phai (goi API /api/admin/gallery/...).
     Nut "Lam moi anh" tai lai anh cho may dang chon.

   - Token duoc luu tam trong trinh duyet (session) cho lan lam viec sau do cung may.

   - Link "Mo trang gallery HTML" hoac duong dan dang /files/<thu_muc>/ mo trang xem anh
     dang danh sach co dien (neu can chia se URL).

   Luu y bao mat: /admin va /api/admin/* chi dung trong mang tin cay; khong dat server
   ra Internet cong khai neu chua co HTTPS / tuong lua chat che.

3) May nhan (client)

   - Giai zip bo may-nhan-goi-client, dam bao co auth.token (cung noi dung may chu).
   - Chay CAI_DAT.bat — nhap IP MAY CHU (vi du 192.168.1.50), chon tu dong khi dang nhap neu can, chon chay client ngay.
   - Hoac chay truc tiep pc-monitor-client.exe sau khi da cau hinh agent.env (MONITOR_BASE_URL / API_URL).

   - Ten hien thi tren /admin: trong agent.env dat DEVICE_DISPLAY_NAME=Ten de doc (Agent ID van la dinh danh luu anh).
   - Tu dong khi dang nhap: CAI_DAT van co the dang ky Task Scheduler; neu agent.env co REGISTER_LOGON_TASK=1 (mac dinh trong bo moi),
     lan chay dau pc-monitor-client.exe cung tu dang ky task mot lan (file register_logon_task.done danh dau da lam).

   - Luu anh moi lan chup vao thu muc tuy chon (tren may nhan): CHON_THU_MUC_LUU_ANH.bat
     hoac agent.env: SCREEN_SAVE_DIR=duong_dan_day_du — khoi dong lai client sau khi doi.
     Van gui anh len may chu nhu cu. Xem them deploy\client-kit\HUONG-DAN.txt.

   Tat / go cai dat: TAT_CLIENT.bat hoac UNINSTALL.bat (xem HUONG-DAN.txt trong bo).


=== B) CHAY KHI DANG CODE (Python, dev) ===

May chu (tu thu muc monitoring\server):

     pip install -r requirements.txt
     python run_server.py

   Mac dinh dev thuong la port 8000 (khac .exe mac dinh 8010). Agent can API_URL tro dung port.

   Web admin (dev):  http://127.0.0.1:8000/admin  - cung cach nhap token nhu muc 2b.

May agent (tu thu muc monitoring\agent):

     pip install -r requirements.txt
     Dat SECRET_TOKEN / auth.token / agent.env theo config.py
     python bot.py                    # co console
   hoac silent (giong client build):

     $env:SILENT_CLIENT="1"; python -c "import os; os.environ['SILENT_CLIENT']='1'; from pc_monitor_bot.app import main_silent; main_silent()"


=== C) KIEM TRA NHANH ===

  - Server da chay: mo  http://<IP_MAY_CHU>:8010/admin  (hoac :8000/admin khi python run_server.py).
  - Dien token -> "Tai danh sach may" -> chon mot dong may -> xem anh ben phai.
  - Neu agent da chay: co it nhat mot may trong danh sach; SCREEN_CAPTURE=1 thi co anh.


=== D) GOI Y LOI THUONG GAP ===

  - 401 / khong gui duoc: token agent khac token server — copy lai auth.token dung file.
  - Admin bao loi 401 / khong tai duoc danh sach: token o o "Token" sai hoac thua khoang
    trang/dong — chi lay dung 1 dong trong auth.token (hoac ADMIN_TOKEN).
  - Khong mo duoc admin tu may khac: firewall / sai IP / server chi lang nghe localhost (kiem tra HOST).
  - Port bi chiem: doi PORT (va API_URL / MONITOR_BASE_URL tren agent cho khop).


=== E) CANH BAO (monitoring-alerter) ===

  Chay dinh ky tren may chu (Task Scheduler) hoac tay:

     cd monitoring\alerter
     $env:MONITOR_DB_PATH = "C:\path\to\monitor.db"
     $env:ALERT_WEBHOOK_URL = "https://..."   # tuy chon: Slack/Discord/custom hook
     python alerter.py

  Hoac dung monitoring-alerter.exe trong deploy\dist, dat MONITOR_DB_PATH tro dung monitor.db
  (cung thu muc monitoring-server.exe neu chay tu do).

  - USB: tren agent DETECT_USB=1 (mac dinh). Cam o USB -> alerter in/webhook type  usb_or_removable.
  - Phan mem khong trong danh sach "duoc phep": trong agent.env dat
      ALLOWED_PROCESS_NAMES=exe1.exe,exe2.exe,...  (liet ke day du exe hop le tren may)
      REGISTERED_PROCESS_CHECK=1
    Khi co process khac danh sach (sau khi bo qua mot so exe he thong), agent gui su kien
    unregistered_software (chi lap lai khi danh sach vi pham thay doi).
  - Trinh duyet qua lau (nghi lam viec rieng - heuristic): chi Windows. Dat
      LONG_BROWSER_ALERT_SECONDS=300
    (vd 5 phut). Dua vao cua so dang nhan tien canh + ten process trinh duyet (Chrome, Edge, ...),
    khong doc URL hay ban phim. Moi phien (cung tab/tieu de) chi canh bao mot lan cho den khi doi cua so.

  - Danh sach web (ten mien) gan day — thu dong, KHONG truy cap vao tung site:
      DNS_CACHE_DOMAINS=1
      DNS_CACHE_DOMAIN_MAX=120
    Agent goi PowerShell Get-DnsClientCache; /admin hien danh sach khi chon may. Day la ten mien da
    phan giai gan day, khong dong nghia dang mo trang do; khong co duong dan URL day du.

  - Luu luong mang (tong tat ca NIC, khong theo tung site) + thoi gian trinh duyet foreground (Windows):
      TRACK_NETWORK_IO=1, TRACK_BROWSER_FOREGROUND=1 — hien tren the may trong /admin.
      NETWORK_ALERT_BYTES_PER_INTERVAL=... (byte / mot chu ky) — canh bao high_network_usage (alerter/webhook).
    Chan hay gioi han bang thong that su: dung proxy/tuong lua trong LAN, khong co trong agent.

  Alerter doc them: alerter\alerter.py (dau file).


Chi tiet bo may nhan: deploy\client-kit\HUONG-DAN.txt va BAT_DAU_O_DAY.txt
